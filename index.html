RULES

RULES AND GUIDELINES

1. No quarreling or use of abuses language

2. No discrimination

3. No posting of anything immoral or indecent content

4. No improper jokes

5. No spamming or private messaging

6. No Unsolicited DMs. Many scammers use DMs to scam, especially. Do not click links in DMs and only listen to official links do not get scammed 7. No posting of links without permission

8. Be polite

9. Be kind and welcoming

10. If an admin or moderator tells you to stop doing something, stop doing that thing. If you get a warning, be careful; you could be banned next time automatically.

11. These rules may be updated periodically as the needs demands. There may be situations not covered by the rules or times when the rule may not fit the situation. If this happens the admins are trusted to handle the situation appropriately.
